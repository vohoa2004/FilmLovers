What are spoilers?
A spoiler is a piece of information which reveals important plot elements (like the ending or a major plot twist), thus "spoiling" a surprise and robbing the viewer of the suspense and enjoyment of the film.
If your review contains spoilers, answer "Yes" to "Does this review contain spoilers?", otherwise it may be deleted.
