User review guidelines
The minimum length for reviews is 600 characters to ensure the most value to our users. Reviews which are padded with junk text will be discarded.
What to include:
Your reviews should focus on the title's content and context. The best reviews include not only whether you liked or disliked a movie or TV-series, but also why. Feel free to mention other titles you consider similar and how this one rates in comparison to them. Reviews that are not specific to the title will not be posted on our site. Please write in English only and note that we do not support HTML mark-up within the reviews.
What not to include:
  • Profanity, obscenities, or spiteful remarks.
  • Avoid unannounced spoilers!
  • Phone numbers, mail addresses, URLs, email addresses.
  • Content in languages other than English
  • Do not include personal opinions on real life events or subject on which a film is based.
Importantly, your review must follow our Code of Conduct.
